Instructions

In this experiment, you will be taking part in a quiz with a group of 10-15 other people.
You will have to answer 100 general knowledge questions.
There are four topics: Geography, Art History, Language recognition, and Weight estimation.

You get one point for each correct answer.
You will be paid based on your points and if you score over 90 points you will receive $20 bonus payment.

If you are unsure of an answer, you can select "Ask Someone Else" to copy the answer of someone else in your group.

When you select "Ask Someone Else" you will be shown how many questions everyone else in your group have answered correctly so far.
You can copy one of their answers by selecting one of the players.

If the person you choose answered correctly, you will also get a point for that question.

If the person you choose answered incorrectly, you will not get a point for that question.

You will not be told if you answered a question correctly or not throughout the quiz. You will be told your total score at the end of the quiz.

Some questions have pictures, others do not.

You have 15 SECONDS to answer each question.

You will now complete three practice questions before you begin the real quiz.
